Our Story

Behind every beautiful tudung is a story — and this is ours.

Welcome to Tudung Kamu

Tudung Kamu started with a simple idea — to create a space where women can find beautiful, comfortable, and easy-to-style hijabs for every moment of their lives.

From rushing to class in the morning, heading to the office, meeting friends for coffee, attending kenduri, or going on weekend trips — we know how important it is to feel confident and comfortable at the same time.

That’s why at Tudung Kamu, every piece is carefully chosen with love. We focus on soft fabrics, breathable materials, flattering cuts, and colours that are easy to match with your wardrobe.

We believe that a tudung is not just something you wear — it is part of who you are. It reflects your personality, your mood, and your confidence.

Our collections are inspired by modern trends, everyday lifestyle, and the beauty of simplicity. We want getting dressed to be fun, effortless, and exciting — not stressful.

At the heart of Tudung Kamu is our love for women who dare to express themselves, who walk confidently in their own style, and who believe that modest fashion can be both elegant and fun.

Our Mission

To empower women with modest wear through high quality materials at an affordable rate while maintaining a sense of comfort

Our Vision

To become a trusted hijab brand loved by women for its comfort, elegance, and modern design.

Our Values

We believe in quality, comfort, and confidence. Every design is made with care, inspired by real women and real lifestyles.

Welcome to Tudung Kamu — where your everyday style becomes something special.
